About us

BLACK OUT has been set with a strategy to provide an outstanding shisha service for hotels and restaurants around the UAE. We have uniquely designed shishas, exclusive heads, accessories and a wide range of tobaccos from traditional Middle East manufacturers to modern worldwide manufacturers with marvelous flavors. The mission of the BLACK OUT company is to stisfy potential customer demands and bring the modernized and personalized experience of smoking shisha.
